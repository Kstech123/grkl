ABOUT

Welcome to Gurukul International School & College, pachora. we were established in june 2008. We conduct CBSE as well as the State Board Syllabus & offers schooling from nursury to class 12th in commerce & science.

As today's world is competitive, it is our responsibility to prepare students for it we are preparing students for many competitive exams like UPSC/MPSC, JEE, NEET from 8th standard onwards.

Our School has a qualitative educational approach based on modern international norms. The School aims to improve academic results as well as character, through a balanced curriculum.
